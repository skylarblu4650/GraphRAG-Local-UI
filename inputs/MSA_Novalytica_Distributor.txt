MASTER SOFTWARE RESELLER & GLOBAL DISTRIBUTION AGREEMENT

EFFECTIVE DATE: January 1, 2025
PARTIES: Novalytica Global, Inc., a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 100 Innovation Way, Santa Clara, CA ("Vendor"), and Global Tech Distribution Corp, a Singapore registered entity ("Distributor").

RECITALS
WHEREAS, Vendor develops and licenses proprietary advanced analytics, machine learning operations (MLOps), and edge intelligence software; and
WHEREAS, Distributor maintains a network of downstream System Integrators (SIs), Value-Added Resellers (VARs), and Managed Service Providers (MSPs);
NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

SECTION 1: DEFINITIONS
1.1 "Authorized SIs" means independent third-party system integrators that have entered into a binding partner agreement with Distributor.
1.2 "Documentation" means the official Vendor user manuals, API references, and deployment guides provided with the Software.
1.3 "End User" means the final corporate or enterprise purchaser of the Software for internal business operations.
1.4 "Novalytica Edge Intelligence" means Vendor's proprietary containerized machine learning environment designed for IoT and remote compute nodes.
1.5 "Telemetry Data" means anonymized usage statistics, hardware performance metrics, and node uptime diagnostics generated by the Software.

SECTION 2: GRANT OF DISTRIBUTION RIGHTS
2.1 Appointment: Vendor hereby appoints Distributor as a non-exclusive, authorized distributor of Vendor's software portfolio in the Asia-Pacific (APAC) region.
2.2 Bundling Rights: Distributor and its Authorized SIs are expressly permitted to bundle Novalytica software licenses with proprietary hardware, third-party software (provided such software does not infringe on Vendor IP), and professional services.
2.3 No Agency: This Agreement does not create an agency, joint venture, or partnership. Distributor acts as an independent contractor.

SECTION 3: FINANCIAL TERMS AND AUDIT RIGHTS
3.1 Payment Terms: Distributor shall remit payment to Vendor within forty-five (45) days (Net-45) from the end of the calendar month in which the Software was provisioned to the Authorized SI.
3.2 Pricing: Pricing shall be governed by the Tiered Channel Price Book (Exhibit A). Vendor reserves the right to adjust pricing with 90 days written notice.
3.3 Audit Rights: Vendor reserves the right, upon 30 days written notice, to audit Distributor's and its Authorized SIs' deployment logs and sales records to ensure compliance with licensing tiers. If an audit reveals underpayment exceeding five percent (5%), Distributor shall pay the shortfall and cover the full cost of the audit.

SECTION 4: TECHNICAL SUPPORT AND MAINTENANCE
4.1 Tier 1 & 2 Support: Distributor or its Authorized SIs shall provide all initial technical support, including installation troubleshooting, configuration, and basic API integration support to the End User.
4.2 Tier 3 Support: Vendor shall provide advanced engineering support strictly to Distributor's certified technical team for undocumented bugs and core platform failures.

SECTION 5: INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY AND BRANDING
5.1 Ownership: Vendor retains all rights, title, and interest in and to the Software, source code, and underlying algorithms.
5.2 Trademarks: Distributor may use Vendor's logos and trademarks strictly in accordance with Vendor's Marketing Guidelines for the sole purpose of promoting the Software.

SECTION 6: WARRANTIES AND INDEMNIFICATION
6.1 Limited Warranty: Vendor warrants that the Software will perform substantially in accordance with the Documentation for 90 days following deployment.
6.2 Indemnification by Vendor: Vendor shall defend Distributor against any third-party claims alleging the Software infringes on a valid patent or copyright.
6.3 Indemnification by Distributor: Distributor shall indemnify Vendor against claims arising from unauthorized modifications to the Software, or breaches of local regulatory compliance by Distributor or its SIs.

SECTION 7: DATA PRIVACY, SECURITY, AND APAC RESIDENCY
7.1 General Privacy: Both parties shall comply with all applicable data protection laws.
7.2 PDPA Compliance: For all deployments within the Republic of Singapore, Distributor shall ensure that End Users and SIs strictly adhere to the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA).
7.3 Telemetry and Residency: Telemetry Data collected by Novalytica Edge Intelligence must be anonymized. For End Users operating in the logistics, smart city, or healthcare sectors within the APAC region, telemetry routing must remain localized. Telemetry Data may not be routed through servers outside of the host country's sovereign borders without explicit, written customer consent and a documented Data Processing Addendum (DPA).

SECTION 8: COMPLIANCE, EXPORT CONTROLS, AND RESTRICTIONS
8.1 Sanctions: Distributor and its Authorized SIs shall comply with all US Department of Commerce export regulations and local international trade sanctions.
8.2 High-Risk Use Cases: The Software may not be deployed in aerospace, nuclear facility management, or life-support medical applications without a specialized High-Risk Deployment License and secondary Vendor audit.
8.3 Commercial Transportation: The Software is fully cleared and optimized for commercial logistics, port authority management, private fleet tracking, and commercial transportation analytics.
8.4 Public Sector Restriction: Notwithstanding Section 8.3, due to ongoing, global compliance audits regarding national critical infrastructure, the resale, sub-licensing, or bundling of Novalytica Edge Intelligence or any underlying analytics modules to any municipal, state, federal, statutory board, or national government entity is strictly prohibited. This restriction applies globally. Exceptions may only be granted if a "Public Sector Exemption Form" is physically signed by Novalytica's Global Vice President of Compliance no less than thirty (30) days prior to the issuance of the End User Purchase Order.

SECTION 9: TERM, TERMINATION, AND LIABILITIES
9.1 Term: This Agreement is valid for three (3) years from the Effective Date.
9.2 Termination for Cause: Either party may terminate if the other commits a material breach that remains uncured for 30 days.
9.3 Immediate Termination and Penalties: Any breach of Section 8 (Compliance, Export Controls, and Restrictions) shall be deemed an incurable material breach. Such breach shall result in the immediate, automated revocation of the offending End User's software license keys. Furthermore, a breach of Section 8.4 will trigger an automatic compliance penalty of $500,000 USD levied against the Distributor per incident, payable within 15 days of breach notification.

SECTION 10: MISCELLANEOUS
10.1 Governing Law: This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.
10.2 Severability: If any provision is found invalid, the remaining provisions will continue in full force.
10.3 Entire Agreement: This document constitutes the entire agreement between the parties regarding the subject matter herein.
