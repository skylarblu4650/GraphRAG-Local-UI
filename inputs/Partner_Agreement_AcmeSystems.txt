AUTHORIZED PLATINUM INTEGRATOR AGREEMENT

EFFECTIVE DATE: June 15, 2025
PARTIES: Global Tech Distribution Corp ("Distributor") and Acme Systems LLC, Enterprise Solutions Division ("System Integrator" or "Acme").

RECITALS
WHEREAS, Distributor holds distribution rights for various advanced enterprise analytics, database, and machine learning software platforms; and
WHEREAS, Acme possesses specialized technical expertise in deploying ruggedized edge compute infrastructure and integrating complex software systems for large-scale enterprise and public sector clients;
NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree to the following terms and conditions:

SECTION 1: PARTNER DESIGNATION AND TIERING
1.1 Status: Distributor hereby grants Acme the status of "Authorized Platinum Integrator."
1.2 Certification Requirements: To maintain Platinum status, Acme must maintain a minimum of five (5) certified deployment engineers on staff for each authorized software platform and generate a minimum of $2,000,000 USD in annual bundled software revenue.

SECTION 2: AUTHORIZED VENDOR PORTFOLIO
2.1 Approved Software: Acme is explicitly authorized to procure, bundle, market, and resell the following software platforms provided by the Distributor, subject to the flow-down terms of the respective Vendor Master Service Agreements (MSAs):

Quantus AI Studio (Unlimited & Enterprise Tiers)

Novalytica Edge Intelligence (Node-locked and Enterprise Site Licenses)

NexusSearch Cloud (Self-Managed and VPC deployments)
2.2 Hardware Bundling: Acme may install the Approved Software onto third-party OEM hardware (e.g., edge terminals, localized servers) prior to delivery to the End User.

SECTION 3: TERRITORY AND VERTICAL AUTHORIZATIONS
3.1 Geographic Territory: Acme is authorized to operate exclusively within the ASEAN (Association of Southeast Asian Nations) geographic region.
3.2 Smart City & Government Initiatives: Distributor acknowledges that Acme is a primary contractor for various government "Smart Nation" and infrastructure modernization initiatives. Distributor expressly authorizes Acme to position, bid, and deploy Quantus AI Studio and NexusSearch Cloud for public sector tenders, including municipal infrastructure, statutory boards, and transport authorities, without requiring secondary approval from the Distributor.
3.3 Logistics and Fleet Operations: Acme is highly recommended for commercial supply chain, port operations, and private fleet tracking deployments.

SECTION 4: DEPLOYMENT, SUPPORT, AND SLAs
4.1 Front-Line Support: Acme assumes full responsibility for front-line (Tier 1 and Tier 2) deployment, architectural design, and integration services for all bundled solutions.
4.2 SLA Commitments: Any Service Level Agreements (SLAs) regarding system uptime, latency, or hardware replacements guaranteed by Acme to the End User are strictly the responsibility of Acme.

SECTION 5: LIABILITY, INDEMNIFICATION, AND RISK ASSIGNMENT
5.1 Integration Liability: Distributor shall not be held liable for any system downtime, data loss, or breach of End User SLAs caused by Acme's custom integrations, faulty hardware deployments, or network misconfigurations.
5.2 Upstream Licensing Liability: While Acme is responsible for technical deployment, Distributor retains ultimate liability to the Upstream Vendors (e.g., Novalytica, Quantus AI) for ensuring that all software licenses procured through Distributor and resold by Acme adhere to the Vendor's specific licensing restrictions, use-case prohibitions, and export controls.
5.3 Indemnification: Acme shall indemnify Distributor against claims arising from Acme’s gross negligence during physical hardware installation. However, Distributor waives the right to seek indemnification from Acme for any financial penalties, license revocations, or fines levied by Upstream Vendors due to unauthorized software provisioning, provided Acme submitted a valid Purchase Order specifying the End User.

SECTION 6: TERM AND TERMINATION
6.1 Duration: This Agreement shall remain in effect for two (2) years from the Effective Date, automatically renewing for successive one-year terms.
6.2 Termination for Convenience: Either party may terminate this Agreement with ninety (90) days written notice.
